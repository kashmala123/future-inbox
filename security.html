Security That Spans Generations

Our Unbreakable Promise to Protect Your Legacy

You're entrusting us with your most precious memories and profound emotions. This is a responsibility we honor with a security architecture designed for permanence. We've built FutureInbox not just to protect your data today, but to guarantee its integrity and delivery for a lifetime and beyond.

Pillars of Trust

Our security framework is built on three foundational pillars that ensure your legacy remains protected through time and technological change.

Absolute Privacy

Your message is sealed with end-to-end encryption from the moment you write it. Only you and your recipient hold the key. No one else, not even us, can ever access its contents.

Digital Fortress

Our infrastructure is built on world-class, redundant cloud platforms, protected by continuous monitoring, intrusion detection, and regular third-party audits to defend against all threats.

The Forever Promise

A legally-binding, independently-funded Trust ensures your message's delivery is not dependent on our company's future, but on a perpetual, time-proof promise.

The Digital VaultYour Message, Sealed at the Source.

The privacy of your message is not just a policy; it is a mathematical certainty. We employ a Zero-Knowledge architecture, meaning we are structurally incapable of viewing your content.

Our Zero-Knowledge Guarantee: From the instant you save your message, it is transformed into unreadable ciphertext on your device. We never see it, we cannot access it, and we cannot decrypt it.

The Time-Proof PromiseEnsuring Delivery Across Generations.

A digital message scheduled for decades from now requires more than technical security; it requires a strategy for permanence.

The FutureInbox Trust: We have legally established and funded an independent trust with a single purpose: to ensure the delivery of all scheduled messages, regardless of our company's future. It's a promise backed by law and resources.

Furthermore, our dedicated preservation team monitors technological evolution, ensuring your encrypted message is migrated to new formats and standards so it will always be readable, 10, 50, or 100 years from now.

Beyond Technology

Our security commitment extends beyond encryption and infrastructure to encompass legal, financial, and operational safeguards.

Legally Binding Trust

Our independently funded Trust is legally obligated to ensure message delivery, regardless of company status or technological changes.

Third-Party Audits

Annual security audits by independent firms ensure we maintain the highest standards of data protection and privacy compliance.

Bug Bounty Program

We collaborate with security researchers worldwide through our bug bounty program to identify and address vulnerabilities.

You Hold the Key to the Vault

Create a Strong Password

Use a unique, complex password for FutureInbox. This is your first line of defense.

Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

MFA is the single most effective way to prevent unauthorized access to your account. We strongly recommend it.

Safeguard Your Recovery Key

Your Recovery Key is the only way to regain access if you forget your password. Treat it like a master key and store it securely offline.

A Critical Responsibility: Because of our Zero-Knowledge design, we cannot reset your password. If you lose your password AND your Recovery Key, your account and all its messages will be permanently and irreversibly lost. This is a necessary trade-off to provide you with true privacy.
