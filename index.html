Your Legacy, Secured for Generations

A Promise Built on Trust

Your memories are priceless, and we treat them that way. Our commitment is to ensure your messages are preserved with uncompromising security and delivered with absolute reliability, no matter how far into the future.

Using AES-256, the same encryption trusted by governments, your messages are locked from the moment you send them until the moment they are opened.

We've established a legal and financial trust to ensure that even if our company ceases to exist, the funds and infrastructure will remain to deliver your messages.
