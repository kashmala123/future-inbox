Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far into the future can I schedule messages?
You can schedule messages up to 10 years into the future. For longer durations, please contact our enterprise solutions team.
Can I edit a message after scheduling?
Yes, you can edit scheduled messages until 24 hours before their delivery time. After that, messages are locked for security reasons.
Is there a limit to the number of messages I can schedule?
Free accounts can schedule up to 10 messages at a time. Premium accounts have unlimited scheduling capabilities.
How secure are my messages?
All messages are encrypted with AES-256 encryption. Messages are stored locally in your browser by default, with optional cloud sync.
Can I cancel a scheduled message?
Yes, you can cancel any scheduled message up to 1 hour before its delivery time through your dashboard.
What happens if a recipient's email changes?
Our system will attempt delivery to the original address. If it fails, you'll be notified and can update the recipient information.
