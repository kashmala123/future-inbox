FutureInbox uses browser storage to save your messages. We don't use cookies for tracking. By using our site, you consent to browser storage.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated:

At FutureInbox, we're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Client-Side Only

All data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

Full Control

You manage all data through your browser settings.

No Tracking

We don't use cookies or analytics. Your messages remain private.

Introduction

Welcome to FutureInbox! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our browser-based application that runs entirely on your device.

Important: FutureInbox is a static website with no backend server. All data processing and storage happens locally in your browser. We never transmit, store, or access your messages on any server.

By using FutureInbox, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access or use our service.

Information Handled Locally

We store minimal data in your browser only:

Message Content

  • Text messages you create
  • Delivery schedules and recipient information
  • Encrypted message data

Browser Storage

  • LocalStorage: Stores your scheduled messages
  • SessionStorage: Temporarily holds current session data

Optional Features

If you use experimental browser-based AI features:

  • Voice recordings processed locally in your browser
  • Text-to-speech conversion data

Client-Side Encryption: All encryption is performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. We don't have access to your encryption keys.

Browser Storage

We use your browser's storage features to provide functionality:

LocalStorage

  • Stores encrypted messages
  • Persists between sessions
  • Capacity: ~5MB per domain

SessionStorage

  • Temporary storage during site use
  • Cleared when browser closes

Managing Storage

You can manage or delete stored data at any time through your browser settings:

Chrome

  1. Open Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Application → Storage
  3. Expand Local Storage
  4. Select futureinbox.com
  5. Click "Clear"

Firefox

  1. Open Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Storage tab
  3. Expand Local Storage
  4. Select futureinbox.com
  5. Click "Delete All"

Safari

  1. Go to Preferences → Privacy
  2. Click "Manage Website Data"
  3. Search for futureinbox.com
  4. Select and click "Remove"

How We Use Your Information

Store your messages locally

Protect content in your browser

Send at scheduled times

Remove after delivery

We use your information exclusively in your browser to:

  • Provide the messaging service
  • Store scheduled messages
  • Encrypt message content
  • Deliver messages at specified times
  • Improve your experience

Important: All processing happens locally in your browser. We never access your messages or store them externally.

Cookies & External Services

We Don't Use Cookies

FutureInbox itself doesn't use any cookies for tracking or functionality.

Third-Party Services

We load these external resources that may collect data:

  • Google Fonts: May collect IP address and font usage stats (Privacy Policy)
  • Font Awesome: May collect icon usage data (Privacy Policy)

Refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

No Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics or any tracking scripts.

Data Security

We implement security measures within your browser:

  • Client-Side Encryption: Messages encrypted before storage using AES-256
  • Browser Security: Leverages your browser's security features
  • No Transmission: Data never leaves your device
  • Automatic Clearing: Messages deleted after delivery

While we implement these measures, browser storage has inherent limitations. We recommend:

  • Using strong device passwords
  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Clearing storage when using shared devices

Data Retention

All data remains in your browser until:

  • Scheduled Messages: Automatically deleted after delivery
  • Manual Deletion: Clear messages anytime via our interface
  • Browser Clearance: Removed when you clear site data

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Use the "Clear All Messages" button in the app
  2. Clear browser storage for futureinbox.com
  3. Disable JavaScript to prevent storage

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, your rights are exercised locally:

  • Access: View all data in browser developer tools
  • Deletion: Clear data using browser settings
  • Correction: Edit messages before delivery
  • Portability: Export your messages as text files

GDPR (EU Residents)

Under GDPR, you have the right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities. Since we don't process data externally, all control remains with you locally.

CCPA (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to request disclosure of data collection and deletion. As we don't collect personal data, these rights are exercised through your browser controls.

EU Representative: For GDPR inquiries, contact our EU representative at gdpr@futureinbox.eu

Children's Privacy

FutureInbox is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Since our service doesn't collect personal data, this primarily applies to ensuring children have parental guidance when using browser storage features.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will provide a summary of key changes

Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify you directly. Please check this page periodically for updates.
